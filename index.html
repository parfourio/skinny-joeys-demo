The Story

Built on
Loyalty.
Period.

Skinny Joey's Cheesesteaks opened its doors on March 29, 2025 — just weeks after the Philadelphia Eagles won the Super Bowl. Philly showed up in full force. The line wrapped around the block and South Broad hasn't been the same since.

"He stood when most folded. He stayed loyal when others switched sides. Some follow. Joey leads — by example, by code, by never backing down."

Founded by Joey Merlino and Joe "Lil Snuff" Perri. Located steps from every major stadium in Philly. Built for the Home Team.

Is Real.

No gimmicks. Just bold flavor, loud energy, and real people.
01

Made Fresh Every Time

Every sandwich is made to order — thin-sliced ribeye, melted sharp provolone or Cooper Sharp, grilled onions, and Aversa's legendary seeded rolls. Nothing pre-made, nothing frozen.

02

Celebrities & Athletes Pull Up

From day one, the biggest names in Philly — and beyond — have come through the doors. Skinny Joey's is already becoming a Philly staple on track to be a national name.

We Can Go Anywhere

We're building something real, with plans to franchise across the country and bring true South Philly flavor to cities coast to coast. This is the beginning — block by block.
