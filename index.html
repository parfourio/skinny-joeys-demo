The Story

Built on
Loyalty.
Loyalty.

Skinny Joey's Cheesesteaks opened its doors on March 29, 2025 — just weeks after the Philadelphia Eagles won the Super Bowl. Philly showed up in full force. The line wrapped around the block, the energy was electric, and South Broad hasn't been the same since.

"He stood when most folded. He stayed loyal when others switched sides. Some follow. Joey leads — by example, by code, and by never backing down."

Founded by Joey Merlino and Joe "Lil Snuff" Perri, Skinny Joey's isn't just a restaurant — it's a movement built on loyalty, hustle, and South Philly pride. Celebrities, pro athletes, and regular Philly people all pull up. This is the beginning — block by block, sandwich by sandwich.
